About CIT

Quality industrial technology education that prepares graduates for practical work and leadership.

Vision
We aspire to be a leading innovative, high-performing college that develops technologically proficient graduates.
Mission
We aspire to be a leading innovative, high-performing college that develops technologically proficient graduates.

Our Goals

Research
Develop qualified students to be effective instruments of inquiry and research with a sense of responsibility and commitment for the improvement of human condition—physical, mental, and spiritual domains.
Productivity
Stimulate and maintain functional and productive activities in various disciplines.
Leadership
Acquire skills and leadership competence in various disciplines in order to accelerate community progress as trained professionals in government and non-government agencies.
Integrity
Adapt skills and internalize intellectual integrity, moral, cultural, and aesthetic values exemplifying ethical norms and professionalism.

Programs We Offer

Building your future with hands-on learning and real-world expertise.

Automotive Technology

Learn the latest in vehicle diagnostics, repair, and maintenance to excel in the automotive industry.

Computer Technology

Develop practical IT and hardware skills in programming, networking, and computer systems maintenance.

Electrical Technology

Gain technical expertise in power systems, wiring, and energy management solutions.

Electronics Technology

Master circuit design, microcontrollers, and automation systems for cutting-edge electronic solutions.

Food Technology

Explore the science of food processing, preservation, and innovation to ensure quality and safety in production.

Garments Technology

Build creative and technical skills in apparel design, pattern making, and textile manufacturing.

Industrial Design Technology

Combine creativity and engineering to design functional, user-centered products and prototypes.

Mechanical Technology

Develop hands-on experience in machining, fabrication, and mechanical system design for modern industries.

Information Technology

Learn software development, data management, and digital solutions to shape the future of technology and innovation.
