Welcome to the College of Industrial Technology

Southern Luzon State University

We provide quality technical education and hands-on training to prepare skilled and industry-ready graduates.

Vision

We aspire to be a leading innovative, high performing college that develops technologically proficient graduates.

Mission

Contribute in the skills development of the Filipino workers innovatively proficient, productive, and versatile in all aspects of technology evolution. Transform students with competence essentials for technological advancement.

Goals and Objectives

  1. Develop qualified students to be effective instruments of inquiry and research with a sense of responsibility and commitment for the improvement of human condition-physical, mental and spiritual domains.
  2. Stimulate and maintain functional and productive activities in various disciplines.
  3. Acquire skills and leadership competence in various disciplines in order to accelerate community progress as trained professionals in government and non-government agencies.
  4. Adapt skills and internalized intellectual integrity, moral, cultural, and aesthetic values exemplifying ethical norms and professionalism.
